Learn Dota 2 Mechanics

Dota 2 is a MOBA game, thus it has common games mechanics like other popular MOBA games such as League of Legend and Mobile Legend. These are the basic mechanics of all MOBA game

  • Heroes movement
  • Attacking
  • Casting spells
  • Using items
  • Fog of War
  • Tower / Creeps aggro
  • Mini map

However, Dota 2 has some interesting mechanics which make it unique from other MOBA games and also more challenging to learn. Here are the mechanics you will only find in Dota 2:

  • Body blocking
  • Denying
  • High ground
  • Cutting / Planting trees
  • Controlled units
  • Heroes turn rate
  • Power runes

Getting familiar with these mechanics takes some time. Thus, it is completely normal to suck at the game if you are new.
