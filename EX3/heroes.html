Learn Dota 2 Heroes

Dota 2 has a huge pool of heroes with difficulty raning from brain-dead to WTF. Here are a bunch of simple heroes I would recommend if you are new to Dota 2:

  • Dragon Knight
  • Wraith King
  • Sven
  • Drow Ranger

You don't need to know how to play every heroes in Dota 2. Just knowing, their abilities and play style can help you understand how to counter or play with the heroes.
