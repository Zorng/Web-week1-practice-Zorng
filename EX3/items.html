Learn Dota 2 Items

Dota 2 has a lot of items that can be purchased by gold (some items are unpurchasable). As you start to play, you can simply follow the item recommendation provided by the game community.

But as you get more experienced you need to itemize according to your role, and game situations. There are a lot of item in game but we can categorize them as the followings:

  • Offensive items
  • Deffensive items
  • Aura items
  • Active items

If you play carry, just buy BKB. Your team will love you for it.
